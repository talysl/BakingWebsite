Top Three Brownies

Brownies Brownies Brownies

INGREDIENTS

1/2 cup of butter, melted
1 cup of tightly packed dark brown sugar
1 egg, lightly beaten
1 teaspoon of vanilla
1/2 teaspoon baking powder
1/8 teaspoon of baking soda
Pinch of salt
1 cup of all-purpose flour
1/3 cup of butterscotch chips (chopped walnuts and chocolate chips are equally tasty)

METHOD

  • 1 Preheat the oven to 350Â°F. Lightly butter and flour an 8X8 pan.

  • Whisk together butter and sugar in a bowl.
  • Add the egg and vanilla extract and whisk.
  • Add the flour, baking soda, baking powder.
  • Add salt, mix it all together.
  • Add the butterscotch chips or other mix-ins.
  • Pour into the pan and spread evenly.
  • Bake for 20-25 minutes
  • Or until a toothpick comes out clean.
  • Allow to cool. Cut into squares and serve.

Whats a brownie?


A chocolate brownie is a flat
baked dessert square developed
in the United States,
at the end of the 19th century. It is a favourite tray bake
across the globe.
It is the cross between a cake
and a soft cookie,completely yummy
Depending on its density,
it may be either fudgy or cakey
and may include nuts, icing, chocolate chips
